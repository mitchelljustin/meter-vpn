Setup WireGuard VPN

Works on Mac, Windows, iOS, Android and Linux.

  • 1. Download and install the WireGuard VPN app.
  • 2. Start the Wireguard VPN app.
  • 3. Click button "Download VPN config" above to download your WireGuard VPN configuration.

  • 4. Import the configuration ZIP file into WireGuard.

  • 5. Click "Activate" on the imported VPN configuration.
    Make sure you've bought enough VPN time or it won't work.
Setup Lightning Payments

Bitcoin is a native currency for the Internet.

Lightning Network is a Bitcoin extension that makes payments instant and anonymous.

  • 1. Buy Bitcoin on an exchange.
    For suggestions on the best exchanges to use look here.
  • 2. Download a Lightning payment wallet app like Zap onto your phone or computer.
  • 3. Open your Lightning wallet and set it up.
  • 4. Send Bitcoin to your Lightning wallet.
    We recommend you send no more than $20 worth of Bitcoin to your Lightning wallet.
  • 5. In your Lightning wallet, open a channel with a well-connected hub such as Yalls or Zap.
